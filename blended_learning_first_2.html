My activity at the university since I met Dr. Muhammad until graduation

I met Dr. Muhammad and explained to him my goals and my passion for science and we talked together about my skills and talent. He asked me a question, “How many percentage of your general activity will enable you to succeed in college?” and my answer to him was that 10% of my general activity is sufficient to succeed in college and get a satisfactory grade. He said to me at the time, "Oh Mohamed, it is not possible to combine two things that are opposite of each other in your mind at the same moment, and the system of study in the college is not compatible with the system of study in advanced universities, and you must finish one of them, which is studying inside the college and obtaining a bachelor's degree, and leaving the other for a while until graduation and to achieve this, only 10% of your general activity will suffice you.” I asked him, What about the rest of my general activity? How do I use it? And his response was as follows: “Do not give anything of it to study with foreign universities, just use it to practice your other talents and skills, such as constantly practicing the game that you have been playing since your childhood, which is kung fu, and also restore your skills in drawing and art and develop it, and You can also participate in scouting, as it is a game that develops many skills, the most important of which is social relations and teamwork, use the rest of 90% of your activity in this matter, because, Mohamed, after you graduate and get your bachelor’s degree, you will return to study with major universities with full strength, with all your energy and your interest; and you will be able to reach your ambition.The college certificate, Mohamed, is only a key to entering the first door towards implementing your ideas and creativity in society, as for your skills and talents that you will develop over the next two years, it will open the rest of the doors for you.” and the meeting between us ended with an agreement on what he advised me to do.

Indeed, I implemented the advice completely in one letter and joined the university team in the scouts. I was responsible for drawing in the roving team, and we camped in the scout camp throughout the school year, training in scouting methods until the time for the University Youth Week competition came, and I had to participate in it because I am an essential member of elected. Unfortunately, the competition schedule conflicted with the schedule of my practical exams in the college, and the Geology Department refused to grant me an exception to take my exam after the end of the competition. I apologized very much to the director of the team, but he grieved and reacted spontaneously to me, and the rest of my colleagues in the team participated in this competition, and they achieved first place at the level of Egyptian universities, and I am very proud of them and myself despite the obstacle that prevented me from participating.

As for sports, I have practiced karate since the age of five and have been practicing kung fu since the age of ten, but I did not have the opportunity to participate in any Federation or participate in any competition. At the university, I implemented Dr. Muhammad ’s advice related to sports, so I went to one of the best kung fu trainers in Egypt, Captain Muhammad Soliman, who was one of the Egyptian national team players and there were those who trained with him who became world champions. I practiced kung fu with Captain Muhammad Soliman for the remaining two years at the university. I was not sure that I would be able to participate in the national championship because it might conflict with the examination schedule, such as what happened in the university’s scouting team, so I did not participate in the Kung Fu Federation and did not enter any tournament. There was also a club in the university that I participated in and practiced swimming for a short period and also practiced taekwondo for two years and participated in the Taekwondo Federation in the hope that I will enter the university team in taekwondo and participate in the university youth week, but there is no need to repeat what happened.

My life during the last two years in college was a full time scout camp and we stayed in tents. When a lecture or lab was scheduled in the college, I would go to receive the lecture or lab and return directly to the camp. When it came time to practice kung fu, I would go to practice for two hours and then go back to camp. When it was time to practice taekwondo, I would also go to training for two hours and then go back to camp. For the record, taekwondo training was during a month, and kung fu training was during the next month, and so on. When it was time to study, I did not study in the camp, but I would go home to study for an hour or two and then go back to sleep in the camp.

In the third year of the college, I got a very good general grade, and in the fourth year I got an excellent general grade, and the general grade in my bachelor's degree was good. I would like to mention one of the subjects that I was required to do in the fourth year, which is the research subject and the article, because I can make the research in any subject I like and in the field I like in the way I like. Indeed, I chose one of the respected doctors, Dr. Ibrahim Korat, and registered with him the research, and it was in the field of earthquakes. At that time, I said to myself, This is an appropriate moment to apply what I have learned with foreign universities in this research, and I suggested to Dr. Ibrahim to make the research practical, not just theoretical talk, and it will be in determining the epicenter of the earthquake and the moment of its launch. I know that this is already known to the world, but in the college we do not practice a practical activity like this. The study is 95% theoretical and my initiation to do an applied research like this is something unique. I asked Dr. Ibrahim to forgive me if I wrote the research by hand and I applied my studies and work with pencil and geometric man. Dr. Ibrahim's response was better than I expected. He said " If you come to me with the research, it is one handwritten paper with one equation of your creativity that will take the full degree ". He asked me not to stress myself too much in completing this research. I told him, yes, I will not waste my interest in the rest of my studies, and the research will not take more than its due. I thought about it and got creative and came up with inventing new equations to locate the epicenter of the earthquake. The research consisted of three papers, not one. I got an excellent grade in this subject, and only two of us got the same grade in the research subject and the article, me and my friend Ahmed Mounir, who was one of the first high school students at the level of the Republic.

It occurred to me to send this research to a foreign scientific journal for evaluation and publication if possible, and this behavior was a pivotal point in my life after graduation. I received the reply from the professor who reviewed the research and there were many hiccups in it that I would never have known. I felt from his words that I was just an amateur and not an expert, despite the fact that I obtained a bachelor’s degree in science, but it did not protect me from his criticism of the research. This idea that I came up with, which is new and unknown to the college, he was saying about it was discovered 100 years ago.

" Oh my God, the creative idea in my community has been known in the outside community for 100 years. How can I pass this hundred years? And how can I be on the same level as the international student and compete with him? ", These were the questions that I got out of my experience with the foreign magazine, and I have sought answers to these questions since my graduation.
